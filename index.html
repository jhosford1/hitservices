Who We Are

We have 10 years of experience installing and maintaining IT infrastructure.

The idea for Hosford IT Services began when Jay Hosford discovered he had a passion for designing networks. He took that initial passion and began to learn to the trade. After cutting his teeth on residential infrastructure, he moved to the commercial, heavy industry and government sectors. Requests for Jay's services began to grow when his clients spread news of it's value through word of mouth. Hosford IT Services has continued to grow with that initial passion continuing to drive it.
